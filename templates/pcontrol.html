Welcome to a dedicated space for parents and educators to support young learners on their financial journey!

Tips for Talking About Money

  • Start early: Introduce basic concepts like saving and spending.
  • Be a role model: Let children see you making financial decisions.
  • Make it fun: Use games and real-life examples.
  • Encourage questions: Create an open dialogue about money.
  • Set clear expectations: Discuss allowances, chores, and goals.
